Research (Papers; Talks)
I am primarily interested in complex geometry and in algebraic geometry. In my projects I utilize Lie-theoretic methods to study the geometry of complex algebraic varieties. The keywords of my research include: contact geometry; rational curves; spherical varieties; nilpotent orbits.
